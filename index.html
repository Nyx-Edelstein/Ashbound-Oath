Rules Reference

Setup

  • Play in offline mode only.
  • New Game+ (NG+1).
  • Remove your Estus Flask after the Undead Asylum. You do not have one. All other healing (Humanity, Divine Blessings, miracles, etc.) is permitted.
  • Choose your class and set the minimum stats needed to two-hand your primary weapon (and meet armor/shield equip load if applicable to ensure you are not over-encumbered). You may level up freely during the run.
  • Start at Firelink Shrine with an empty inventory except for your starting gift, class items, and your rolled equipment.

Goal

  • Defeat every boss with a victory screen before Ornstein & Smough, then defeat O&S.
  • Four Kings is optional.
  • No quit-out cheese. No glitches. No sequence breaking.

Required Bosses (in logic, before O&S)

  • Taurus Demon
  • Bell Gargoyles
  • Capra Demon
  • Gaping Dragon
  • Quelaag
  • Iron Golem
  • Moonlight Butterfly
  • Sif, the Great Grey Wolf
  • Ceaseless Discharge
  • Pinwheel
  • Crossbreed Priscilla
  • Stray Demon
  • Dark Sun Gwyndolin
  • Ornstein & Smough — final goal
  • (Optional) Four Kings

Weapon

  • You receive a primary weapon and a secondary weapon (backup). The secondary is always a safe normal weapon in case your primary proves unviable.
  • You may not equip any other weapon in your right hand for the entire run.
  • All weapons start fully upgraded along whatever upgrade path you choose.
  • The Pyromancy Flame starts at +5 Ascended.
  • For any build, you may use an unupgraded Pyromancy Flame with Power Within in your off-hand once you obtain the items in your run.
  • If your weapon is a bow, you may give yourself 999 basic Arrows.
  • If your weapon is a crossbow, you may give yourself 999 basic Bolts.
  • If your weapon is a flame, catalyst, or talisman, you may start with 40 in your main stat and one damaging spell whose stat requirement is ≤ 40.

Shield (if enabled)

  • Random Shield: You may only use the rolled shield. No other shields may be equipped.
  • No Shield (Two-Hand or Dual Wield): You may not equip any shield. Two-hand your weapon or dual wield any off-hand weapon you scavenge during your run.
  • No Shield (Caster Off-Hand): You may not equip any shield. Use a catalyst, talisman, or pyromancy flame in your off-hand with a starting spell.
  • No Restrictions: Equip any shield freely. Shields are separate from left-hand weapons.

Armor (if enabled)

  • You may not equip any other armor found in your run.
  • The helm may be unequipped, or swapped for special items once obtained in your run (Dusk Crown, Masks, etc).
  • You may give yourself the minimum Endurance to stay under 100% equip load (avoid over-encumbrance).

Rerolls

  • Each category (primary weapon, secondary weapon, shield, armor) may be rerolled up to 2 times using the 🎲 button after sealing your Oath.
